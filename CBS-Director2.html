Executive Director

Mr Thabo Makhubela

(Executive Director: Funding)
thabo@sa-cbs.co.za

Profile

Mr Given Thabo Makhubela holds a BCom Degree in Business Management with majors in Business Management & Economics from the University of Venda. He also graduated from the University of Limpopo (Turfloop) with a Post-Graduate Certificate in Education specialising with business studies and economics. He worked in a well-known insurance company (AVBOB) as a financial Advisor from 2011 to 2013, moreover he worked as an educator at secondary level for the period of 2 Years, He then joined the tertiary education sector which includes; Ekurhuleni West College (Kempton Park), PC Training & Business College (Kempton Park) and Central Johannesburg TVET College, where He lectured Business Studies, Accounting, Marketing Management, and Economics, because of his hard work and determination for the year 2016 he was appointed by the department of higher education as a chief marker and senior moderator for the Gauteng region. Mr Makhubela currently occupies a portfolio of Executive Director: Funding at CBS Pty (Ltd) since October 2016, responsible for overseeing the awarding of funds, setting up the funding committee, coming up with different funding strategies, and the development and implementation of the funding policy.
