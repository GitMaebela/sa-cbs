Our Programmes

Funding

We fund the deserving start-ups from all walks of life, especially people from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Training & Development

We provide skills which include: creating a Business plan, Business proposal; Company Registration; Market Research; Customer Care/Service; Financial Management; Investment; and Analysis of south African and global economies.

Mentorship

Each of our beneficiaries are assigned a competent business mentor, who helps in terms of identifying business opportunities and providing necessary supervision.

Capacity Building

We host workshops and business seminars which are targeted at passionate entreprenuers, whereby they are capacitated, motivated and equipped with up-to-date information as far as the global economy is concerned.

About CBS

Background

96% of startups in South Africa say they have had little assistance (financial or otherwise) from the national government - in building their businesses.

Mission

To help emerging entrepreneurs to kick start their businesses by providing capital, training, mentorship and capacity building programmes.
