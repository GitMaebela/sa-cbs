Funding

Commencement Business Solutions only assists with capital not cash, meaning we buy equipment, stock and pay rent if applicable etc. and all that you might need to start your business.

Training & Development

Commencement Business Solutions aims at providing skills which include the; drafting of a business plan, business proposal, company registration, market research, customer service, financial management, budgeting, investment.

Mentorship


All beneficiaries are assigned competent business mentors, who will help in terms of identifying business opportunities and providing necessary supervision.

Capacity Building


Commencement Business Solutions holds workshops and business seminars whereby beneficiaries are equipped with up-to-date information as far as business and the South African economy is concerned.
